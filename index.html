Greetings, traveller!

Take a seat, let me tell you something about myself.

As a child I had a passion for computers and video games, and I played hundreds if not thousands of them. I asked myself “How does one make a game?”. Through my curiosity I experimented, first with interactive comic books I created for my friends in elementary school, and then with many simple projects in different game engines. I watched countless documentaries, tutorials and interviews.

My "serious" steps towards my career began when I attended game design courses by Code Week Montenegro and Amplitudo Akademija. I used these opportunities to improve my skills, gain experience in 2D and 3d game design, and build up my portfolio.

In 2021, I was selected for the PlayUK Western Balkans mentorship scheme. I spent 8 weeks working alongside UK game industry professionals, refined my skills, and learned about working in the game industry.

Making games is something I truly love. Designing atmospheric, memorable experiences with good mechanics, believeable characters and dramatic stories is something that I strive for. When a player feels the same emotional impact and joy that I have felt many times during these years of playing and creating games, I will know that I have succeeded.

I am always looking to improve and gain new experience. I look forward to working on ever bigger projects with like-minded and ambitious people.

Who knows, perhaps one day we will get to work together? For additional information you can find my contacts on the bottom of the page.
